Butter Chicken Curry

Here is a step by step process on how to prepare delicious butter chicken curry at home even if you dont have any prior experience in cooking !

Ingredients

  • 250gm Chicken breast
  • 3 onions
  • 50gm butter(salted)
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Prepare the curry
  3. Eat it all
  4. Throw the bones in the dustbin
